***/bazaar*** \
Gets the default [namespace]({{site.baseurl}}/posts/bazaar-namespace) specified in "plugins/Bazaar/config.yml"and executes
```yaml
/bz open <namespace>
```
for the player.
Permission: *bazaar.open*
